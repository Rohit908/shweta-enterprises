Who We Are

Shweta Enterprises — Built on Trust

Shweta Enterprises is a reliable manufacturer and supplier of high-quality uniforms and textile solutions. We specialize in school uniforms, housekeeping uniforms, and security uniforms, designed to provide comfort, durability, and a professional appearance.

With a strong commitment to quality and customer satisfaction, we ensure that every product meets the highest standards of stitching, fabric selection, and finishing.

In addition to uniforms, we also offer custom label manufacturing, helping businesses create a strong brand identity through well-designed and durable labels.

🎯

Our Mission

To provide high-quality, comfortable, and durable school, housekeeping, and security uniforms, along with reliable custom label solutions. We are committed to delivering excellence through quality materials, skilled workmanship, timely delivery, and customer-focused service.

🔭

Our Vision

To become a trusted and leading name in the uniform and textile industry, known for quality, reliability, and innovation. We aim to expand our reach by continuously improving our products, adopting modern techniques, and setting new standards in uniform manufacturing.

Our Founder

Raju Prasad

Founded in 2007, Shweta Enterprises began with a simple vision: to deliver uniforms that combine quality, comfort, and professionalism. Under the leadership of Raju Prasad, the company has grown into a trusted name for schools, corporates and organisations across Mumbai.

His commitment to precision craftsmanship and customer-first values continues to drive every product that leaves our facility.
